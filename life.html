Conway's Game of Life

Instructions

Click and/or drag on a dead cell to enable cells or on a live cell to disable cells.

Rules

  • Any living cell with less than 2 neighbors will die.
  • Any living cell with more than 3 neighbors will die.
  • Any dead cell with exactly 3 neighbors will become alive.
